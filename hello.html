        Changes to Blackboard on Saturday 27 January 2024

        As part of our planned upgrade of the Blackboard interface, there will be some disruption to this service.

        We expect Blackboard to be unavailable for a 30-minute period at some point between 8am and 2pm GMT on Saturday, 27 January 2024. During this time, anyone using Blackboard may see some graphical changes as the upgrade takes effect.

        The upgrade will provide improvements to the Blackboard homepage design and several new features.

        There will be no changes to learning materials, assessments or the Blackboard tools, and no action is required by staff or students.

        For further information please see the articles on StaffNet News and Student News.
